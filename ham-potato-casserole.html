Ham, Potato, and Broccoli Casserole

Description

This convenient dish is a nutrient packed option for anyone short on time and big on flavor.
You can enjoy it from the comfort of your home after a long day at work, or prepare it with
extra spirit around loved ones when the holidays are approaching. Adding left-over protein
options, as well as extra cheese would make for an appeasing addition.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen French fries
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
  • 1 ½ cups cooked, cubed ham
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can milk
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray. Cover bottom of dish with layer
    of French fries. Add a layer of broccoli, then sprinkle ham evenly over broccoli.
    In a small bowl, mix together soup, milk and mayonnaise. Pour this mixture evenly
    over ingredients in baking dish and sprinkle with cheese.
  3. Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
